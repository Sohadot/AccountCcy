# AccountCcy.com SEO Policy

## Purpose

This document governs SEO strategy for AccountCcy.com.

SEO must serve the asset’s authority, not dilute it.

The goal is not traffic volume.

The goal is to build durable search authority across institutional terminology that strengthens AccountCcy.com as a sovereign-grade reference asset.

---

## Governing SEO Thesis

**SEO is a servant of authority.**

AccountCcy.com must not chase traffic that weakens its positioning.

Every ranking page must strengthen the thesis:

**AccountCcy.com is the chain of custody for monetary truth inside enterprise finance.**

---

## SEO Objectives

The SEO objectives are:

1. build authority around account currency terminology
2. own narrow professional search intent
3. support the Currency State Control Framework
4. make the CCY State Chain discoverable
5. attract CFOs, controllers, ERP architects, and enterprise finance professionals
6. create buyer legibility through professional search presence
7. support future diagnostic and premium report monetization
8. compound acquisition value over time

Traffic alone is not the objective.

Qualified authority is the objective.

## Search Intent Classes

AccountCcy.com should serve five search intent classes.

### 1. Definition Intent

Users want to understand a term.

Example:

- what is account currency
- ccy meaning finance
- reporting currency definition

Content response:

Clear definition, professional context, relationship to the framework.

---

### 2. Comparison Intent

Users want to distinguish related concepts.

Example:

- transaction currency vs account currency
- functional currency vs reporting currency
- ledger currency vs reporting currency

Content response:

Structured comparison, consequences of confusion, internal links.

---

### 3. ERP Intent

Users want to understand behavior inside systems.

Example:

- account currency in SAP
- Oracle reporting currency
- ERP multi-currency configuration

Content response:

Reference-oriented explanation, no false affiliation, no copied vendor documentation.

---

### 4. Risk Intent

Users want to understand consequences.

Example:

- foreign currency revaluation
- currency translation accounting
- account reconciliation currency risk

Content response:

Explain risk, controls, and relationship to account currency governance.

---

### 5. Strategic Intent

Users want higher-level interpretation.

Example:

- CFO multi-currency accounting risk
- financial close currency risk
- enterprise finance currency control

Content response:

CFO-level framing, governance implications, diagnostic path.

## Page Title Rules

Titles must be clear, professional, and search-aligned.

Preferred examples:

- What Is Account Currency?
- Transaction Currency vs Account Currency vs Reporting Currency
- The Currency State Control Framework
- The CCY State Chain
- Account Currency in ERP Systems
- CFO Guide to Hidden Account Currency Risk
- Currency Translation and FX Revaluation Explained

Avoid:

- clickbait titles
- exaggerated titles
- domain-sale language
- vague thought leadership titles
- keyword-stuffed titles

---

## Meta Description Rules

Meta descriptions should:

- state the page topic clearly
- include relevant terminology naturally
- preserve institutional tone
- avoid hype
- avoid sales language
- connect the topic to account currency control where appropriate

They should not overpromise.

---

## URL Rules

URLs must be:

- lowercase
- stable
- readable
- clean
- conceptually precise

Preferred:

- `/what-is-account-currency/`
- `/ccy-state-chain/`
- `/account-currency-in-erp/`
- `/transaction-vs-account-vs-reporting-currency/`

Avoid:

- long keyword-stuffed URLs
- dates in permanent pages
- `.html` in public-facing canonical URLs
- unclear abbreviations unless necessary

---

## Internal Linking Rules

Every page should link to relevant higher-order pages:

- Homepage
- Chain of Custody for Monetary Truth
- Currency State Control Framework
- CCY State Chain
- What Is Account Currency?
- Glossary

Internal links must serve conceptual clarity.

They should not exist only for SEO manipulation.

---

## Content Depth Rules

Ranking pages must be reference-grade.

Each SEO page should include:

- definition or thesis
- professional context
- role inside the CCY State Chain
- risks or consequences
- related concepts
- internal links
- careful terminology
- no unsupported claims

Thin SEO content is prohibited.

---

## Evidence and Claim Rules

SEO pages must not sacrifice precision for ranking.

Claims involving accounting standards, ERP systems, vendors, revaluation, translation, consolidation, or audit must be carefully worded.

Original AccountCcy.com concepts must be identified as framework language, not official standards.

Vendor-related pages must not imply official affiliation.

---

## Prohibited SEO Tactics

The following are prohibited:

- keyword stuffing
- mass AI content
- doorway pages
- copied vendor documentation
- thin glossary pages
- auto-generated low-quality pages
- irrelevant finance topics
- forex trading content
- crypto traffic capture
- affiliate-driven pages
- misleading titles
- unsupported authority claims

---

## Sitemap Policy

The sitemap must include only public, canonical, indexable pages.

It should not include:

- draft files
- internal governance documents
- test pages
- duplicate pages
- non-public assets
- utility files not meant for indexing

Every sitemap URL should correspond to a page that strengthens the asset.

---

## Canonical Policy

Each public page must have one canonical URL.

Canonical URLs should use:

`https://accountccy.com/path/`

The homepage canonical should be:

`https://accountccy.com/`

Avoid canonical conflicts.

## SEO Success Metrics

SEO success should be measured by:

- rankings for professional terminology
- impressions from relevant enterprise finance queries
- engagement from qualified users
- growth of glossary authority
- inbound links from serious sources
- diagnostic tool usage
- professional newsletter signups
- premium report conversions
- strategic acquisition inquiries

Raw traffic alone is not success.

---

## Final SEO Test

Before targeting any keyword, ask:

**Will ranking for this keyword strengthen AccountCcy.com as the chain of custody for monetary truth inside enterprise finance?**

If yes, the keyword may be considered.

If no, ignore it.

SEO must serve the asset.

The asset must not serve SEO.
