Account Currency vs Ledger Currency

Professionals searching for account currency vs ledger currency are usually separating account-level custody from entity-level measurement. Account currency governs how balances are assigned and reconciled at State 04; ledger currency hosts the trial balance and measurement shell at State 05. The distinction matters for ERP close design, revaluation populations, subledger mapping, and audit reconstruction.

Governing distinction

Account currency controls balance custody. Ledger currency hosts measured books.

AccountCcy.com comparison reference

Search intent answer

Account-level custody versus ledger-level measurement

Account currency is granular: it attaches currency identity to account populations and reconciliations.

Ledger currency is structural: it hosts entity books, trial balance integrity, and measurement continuity.

The two layers interact during close, but a chart-level custody setting is not the same as the measurement currency of the ledger.

Operational consequences of the distinction

ERP consequence: account master data, posting logic, and ledger settings may each hold different parts of the truth.

Close consequence: revaluation batches can be wrong if account-currency populations are assumed from ledger currency alone.

Reconciliation consequence: a trial balance can tie while account-level foreign populations remain poorly explained.

Reporting consequence: ledger totals may obscure the account-currency custody decisions that shaped FX outcomes.

Risk boundary

Where confusion creates risk

Confusion creates risk when teams use local currency, functional currency, ledger currency, and account currency interchangeably.

The failure pattern is a clean trial balance that cannot be replayed back to controlled account populations.

FAQ

Practical comparison questions

  • Is ledger currency just another name for account currency? No. Account currency is account-level custody; ledger currency is the measurement shell for books.

  • Which one drives the trial balance? Ledger currency hosts trial balance measurement, while account currency shapes the populations feeding it.

  • Why does this matter for revaluation? Revaluation depends on controlled populations, not only ledger-level labels.
