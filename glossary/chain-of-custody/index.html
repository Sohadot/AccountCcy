Reference Definition

What Chain of Custody means inside enterprise finance.

Chain of custody names the institutional discipline of preserving continuity as monetary signals transform—from market quotation through obligation creation, settlement movement, account assignment, ledger measurement, reporting translation, consolidation assembly, and audit defensibility. Break custody anywhere and truth claims lose footing.

Operational meaning

Chain of Custody inside enterprise finance operations.

Custody is operationalized through contracts, rate policies, TMS confirmations, ERP configuration, period-end controls, consolidation methodologies, and documentary standards. Each hop must reference the prior hop; otherwise forensic reconstruction replaces governance.

Position in Chain

The CCY State Chain is AccountCcy’s concrete articulation of monetary custody across eight currency states.

Why it matters

Chain of Custody as a control concept

Custody gaps are where FX disputes, close delays, restatements, and governance escalations concentrate.

Risk if misunderstood

Where misunderstanding creates distortion

Assuming custody equals logging transactions ignores configuration-state custody—the dominant failure pattern in multi-currency ERP environments.

Distinctions

Do not confuse this term with adjacent currency identities.

Chain of custody is broader than an audit trail artifact: it includes commercial, treasury, and configuration decisions that audit trails only reflect if designed intentionally.

Custody question

Does every custody hop name its predecessor and successor states without ambiguity?
