# AccountCcy.com Content Governance

## Purpose

This document governs the content standards for AccountCcy.com.

It defines what may be written, what must be avoided, how claims should be handled, and how content must support the asset’s sovereign-grade positioning.

AccountCcy.com must not become a generic finance content site.

It must become a reference-grade institutional asset.

---

## Governing Content Thesis

All content must serve the thesis:

**AccountCcy.com is the chain of custody for monetary truth inside enterprise finance.**

Every page must help explain, structure, apply, or defend this thesis.

Content exists to build authority, not volume.

---

## Content Objectives

Content must strengthen one or more of the following:

- monetary truth custody
- Currency State Control Framework
- CCY State Chain
- account currency control
- multi-currency accounting understanding
- ERP currency architecture
- CFO-level risk awareness
- financial close and reconciliation relevance
- audit-defensible reporting logic
- professional SEO authority
- future monetization quality
- strategic acquisition value

If content does not strengthen one of these, it should not be published.

---

## Content Tone

Content must be:

- institutional
- precise
- calm
- serious
- reference-grade
- professionally useful
- conceptually strong
- structurally clear
- defensible
- restrained

Content must not sound:

- desperate
- promotional
- retail
- speculative
- hype-driven
- clickbait
- vague
- cheaply luxurious
- domain-sales oriented
- mass-market finance oriented

---

## Content Categories

### Foundational Content

Purpose:

To define the asset’s doctrine and framework.

Examples:

- The Chain of Custody for Monetary Truth
- Currency State Control Framework
- CCY State Chain
- What Is Account Currency?

Foundational content must be deep, precise, and internally linked.

---

### Reference Content

Purpose:

To serve professional search intent and build topical authority.

Examples:

- Account Currency Definition
- Transaction Currency vs Account Currency
- Functional Currency vs Reporting Currency
- Ledger Currency Explained
- Currency Translation Accounting
- FX Revaluation

Reference content must answer real professional questions while strengthening the asset’s conceptual hierarchy.

---

### Application Content

Purpose:

To apply the framework to professional contexts.

Examples:

- Account Currency in ERP Systems
- CFO Guide to Hidden Account Currency Risk
- Account Currency and Financial Close
- Account Currency and Reconciliation
- Account Currency and Consolidation

Application content should connect framework logic to real enterprise finance behavior.

---

### Tool-Support Content

Purpose:

To support future diagnostic and monetization layers.

Examples:

- Currency State Diagnostic explanation
- maturity model documentation
- diagnostic report interpretation
- checklist pages
- framework licensing material

Tool-support content must strengthen methodology, not create gimmicks.

---

### Acquisition-Support Content

Purpose:

To make the asset legible to strategic buyers.

Examples:

- Strategic Acquisition
- Category Positioning
- Institutional Use Cases
- Framework Licensing

Acquisition-support content must remain restrained.

It must not resemble a domain sales pitch.

---

## Evidence Discipline

AccountCcy.com may create original conceptual language.

However, public claims must distinguish between:

- established accounting terminology
- ERP configuration behavior
- reporting practice
- structured financial data language
- original AccountCcy.com framework interpretation

The site must never present original framework language as if it were an official accounting standard.

Claims connected to accounting standards, ERP systems, financial reporting, revaluation, translation, consolidation, and audit must be written with care.

---

## Source Discipline

When public pages rely on institutional facts, they should be grounded in credible sources such as:

- accounting standards bodies
- ERP documentation
- financial reporting references
- professional accounting education
- recognized enterprise finance documentation
- reputable audit, treasury, or accounting sources

Vendor references must not imply endorsement or affiliation.

The asset must remain independent.

---

## Prohibited Content

The following content types are prohibited:

- forex trading tips
- crypto speculation
- get-rich financial content
- low-grade affiliate content
- generic personal finance
- mass-market currency conversion pages
- display advertising content
- shallow SEO articles
- scraped or rewritten vendor documentation
- generic AI-generated finance articles
- domain sale announcements
- hype-driven investment language
- unsupported claims about standards or vendors

---

## Original Framework Rule

The following terms belong to AccountCcy.com’s original conceptual architecture:

- Currency State Control Framework
- CCY State Chain
- Chain of Custody for Monetary Truth
- Audit Reality
- Account Currency Control Layer

These must be used consistently.

They must be explained carefully.

They must not be diluted across unrelated topics.

---

## Definition Rule

Definitions must be:

- concise
- precise
- context-aware
- internally linked
- non-speculative
- professionally credible

A definition should not merely explain a term.

It should place the term inside the AccountCcy.com framework.

---

## Page Quality Rule

Every major page should include:

- clear thesis
- precise definitions
- professional context
- relationship to the CCY State Chain
- risk or consequence if misunderstood
- internal links to related pages
- restrained language
- evidence-aware wording
- no unsupported overclaims

---

## Content Length Rule

Content should be long enough to be authoritative but not artificially long.

Depth matters more than length.

A page should end when the concept is sufficiently explained.

No padding.

No filler.

No repetition for SEO.

---

## AI Use Rule

AI may assist with drafting, structure, editing, and consistency.

AI must not:

- invent unsupported claims
- weaken the doctrine
- turn the asset into generic finance content
- imitate vendor documentation
- overstate accounting conclusions
- create pages that do not serve the asset
- introduce new frameworks without approval

Human strategic control remains primary.

---

## Commercial Content Rule

Commercial content must preserve dignity.

Allowed:

- premium reports
- diagnostic interpretation
- professional briefings
- framework licensing
- training resources
- selective institutional partnerships

Not allowed:

- aggressive sales pages
- discount language
- countdown timers
- low-trust lead forms
- domain marketplace framing
- banner-heavy sponsorship

---

## Final Content Test

Before publishing any content, ask:

**Does this strengthen AccountCcy.com as the chain of custody for monetary truth inside enterprise finance?**

If yes, it may proceed.

If no, it should be rejected.

If uncertain, return to the foundation doctrine.
