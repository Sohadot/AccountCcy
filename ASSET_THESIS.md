Enterprise finance does not merely manage money.

It manages monetary truth.

A currency may begin as a market rate, become a transaction, settle as cash, enter an account, move into a ledger, appear in a report, consolidate across entities, and finally face audit scrutiny.

AccountCcy.com exists to map that transformation.

The central insight is:

**Money does not become accounting truth automatically. It becomes truth through custody.**
