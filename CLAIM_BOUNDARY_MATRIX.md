# AccountCcy.com Claim Boundary Matrix

## Document Status

This document defines which types of claims AccountCcy.com may make, which claims require restraint, and which claims are prohibited.

It is a trust-protection file.

---

## Claim Boundary Table

| Claim Type | Allowed Posture | Required Boundary | Prohibited Form |
|---|---|---|---|
| Original framework claim | "AccountCcy defines..." | Identify as AccountCcy framework language. | "This is the official standard." |
| Definition claim | "In enterprise finance usage..." | Keep concise and link to glossary or framework. | Universal claims that ignore context. |
| ERP behavior claim | "ERP systems may..." or "common ERP patterns..." | Avoid vendor-specific instruction unless sourced. | "Configure your ERP this way." |
| Accounting standards claim | "Professional standards use concepts such as..." | Avoid entity-specific conclusions. | Determining functional currency or compliance. |
| Audit claim | "Audit defensibility depends on evidence..." | State that this is reference content, not audit advice. | "This will satisfy auditors." |
| Risk claim | "Weak custody can create risk..." | Explain mechanism, avoid certainty where context matters. | Guaranteed material misstatement claims. |
| Diagnostic claim | "Produces a maturity signal..." | Non-certifying, local-first, educational. | Compliance score, audit certification, assurance opinion. |
| Monetization claim | "May support a premium report..." | Keep commercial layer secondary. | Urgency, guaranteed outcome, cheap sales copy. |
| Acquisition claim | "May be considered for strategic acquisition..." | Institutional alignment only, no price anchoring. | Domain sale pitch, make-offer language, public desperation. |
| External evidence claim | "Recorded external signal..." | Verify and document source. | Inflated authority from weak mentions. |

---

## Green Claims

These are generally acceptable:

- AccountCcy.com is a reference asset.
- AccountCcy.com uses an original CCY State Chain framework.
- Account currency is treated here as a custody control point.
- The diagnostic is a non-advisory maturity tool.
- The site provides educational and reference material.
- Evidence discipline strengthens audit defensibility.

---

## Yellow Claims

These require careful wording:

- Any statement involving accounting standards.
- Any statement involving ERP configuration behavior.
- Any statement involving audit outcomes.
- Any statement involving financial reporting treatment.
- Any statement involving professional best practice.
- Any statement involving market inevitability or buyer value.

Use qualified language:

- may
- can
- often
- in many enterprise contexts
- should be evaluated by qualified professionals
- under applicable standards and policies

---

## Red Claims

These are prohibited:

- AccountCcy is an official accounting standard.
- The diagnostic certifies compliance.
- The diagnostic replaces audit, tax, legal, accounting, treasury, or ERP advice.
- AccountCcy is affiliated with any ERP vendor without proof.
- A buyer will inevitably acquire the asset.
- The asset cannot be copied in any way.
- Any price, guaranteed valuation, or guaranteed revenue claim.
- Any claim that a page or tool will satisfy auditors.

---

## Final Claim Rule

If a serious CFO, controller, ERP architect, auditor, or strategic buyer could reasonably challenge the claim, narrow it before publication.
