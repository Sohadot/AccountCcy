CCY State Chain · State 03

Settlement Currency

State 03 captures wires, ACH-style rails, on-behalf payments, and netting settlements. When settlement diverges from transaction denomination, FX gains or losses begin their journey toward postings defenders must later narrate.

Cash Reality

Liquidity execution intersecting accounting recognition.

Treasury executes here daily while controllers await mirrored postings. Misalignment between banking cutoff calendars and ERP value dates produces phantom breaks controllers mistaken attribute purely to rate noise.

Custody Question

Which currency actually clears cash — and does documentation tie every movement back to its originating obligation currency?

Position in Chain

Settlement Currency inside the chain of custody.

Settlement bridges commercial obligation identity toward accounting custody. Weak handshake between TMS feeds and ERP cash modules fractures traceability before accounts ever assign custody identities.
