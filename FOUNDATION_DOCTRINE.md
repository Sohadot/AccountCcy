## 11. The CCY State Chain

The CCY State Chain is the core doctrine of AccountCcy.com.  
It is the asset’s primary intellectual architecture.  
It consists of eight states.

**State 01 — Quoted Currency**  
Currency as a market price. At this stage, currency is a rate signal. It has not yet entered a transaction, an account, a ledger, a report, or an audit trail. It is price, not record.

**State 02 — Transaction Currency**  
The currency in which a financial event is denominated. This is where obligation begins.

**State 03 — Settlement Currency**  
The currency in which payment or settlement occurs. The settlement currency may differ from the transaction currency. This divergence can create foreign exchange consequences, operational complexity, and reconciliation requirements.

**State 04 — Account Currency**  
The currency assigned to the account in which the transaction is recorded or maintained.  
**This is the critical control point.**  
This is where external monetary movement enters accounting custody.  
This is the account currency moment.  
This is where AccountCcy.com lives.

**State 05 — Ledger Currency**  
The currency in which the primary ledger or accounting books are maintained, often aligned with the entity’s functional currency depending on accounting framework and ERP configuration.

**State 06 — Reporting Currency**  
The currency in which financial results are presented for management, regulatory, investor, or internal reporting purposes.

**State 07 — Consolidation Currency**  
The group-level currency into which multiple entities, ledgers, and reporting structures are consolidated. This is where multi-entity financial truth is unified.

**State 08 — Audit Reality**  
The final defensible state. This is the currency reality that survives reconciliation, documentation, compliance review, board scrutiny, and audit examination. Audit Reality is not merely a number. It is a number that can be defended.
